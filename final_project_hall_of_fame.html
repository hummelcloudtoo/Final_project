Tom Brady (Patriots)

The greatest quarterback in NFL history, with more Super Bowls than any individual team. Brady led the Patriots to six Super Bowl victories and redefined the meaning of success.

  • 7× Super Bowl Champion
  • 3× NFL MVP
  • 5× Super Bowl MVP
  • All-Time Passing Touchdowns Leader
  • All-Time Passing Yards Leader

Larry Bird (Celtics)

One of the most complete players in NBA history, "Larry Legend" led the Celtics to three championships and was known for his clutch shooting, trash talk and fierce competitiveness.

  • 3× NBA Champion
  • 3× NBA MVP
  • 12× All-Star
  • Member of 1992 “Dream Team”

David Ortiz (Red Sox)

Known as “Big Papi,” Ortiz helped break the 86-year drought and became a postseason legend beloved across New England.

  • 3× World Series Champion
  • 10× All-Star
  • 2013 World Series MVP
  • 541 Career Home Runs

Bobby Orr (Bruins)

Bobby Orr revolutionized hockey with unmatched skating and offensive dominance, redefining the role of the defenseman forever.

  • 2× Stanley Cup Champion
  • 8× Norris Trophy Winner
  • 2× Conn Smythe Trophy Winner

Bill Russell (Celtics)

One of the greatest champions ever, Russell led the Celtics dynasty and was a historic voice for civil rights and equality.

  • 11× NBA Champion
  • 5× MVP
  • 12× All-Star
  • NBA Lifetime Achievement Award

Ted Williams (Red Sox)

The last .400 hitter and one of the greatest in baseball history, Williams’ legacy lives on in Fenway lore.

  • 2× AL MVP
  • 19× All-Star
  • 2× Triple Crown Winner
  • .344 Career Average

Rob Gronkowski (Patriots)

One of the most dominant tight ends ever, Gronk combined power, athleticism, and humor to become a New England icon.

  • 4× Super Bowl Champion
  • 5× Pro Bowler
  • 4× First-Team All-Pro
  • 92 Career TDs

Ray Bourque (Bruins)

One of the greatest defensemen in NHL history, Bourque built a legacy of longevity, consistency, and excellence during more than two decades with the Bruins.

  • NHL All-Time Leader in Points by a Defenseman
  • 19× All-Star
  • 5× Norris Trophy Winner
  • 21 Seasons with the Bruins
